# Roadmap

## Upcoming Features

### QR Attendance Export (Enhancement / Feature) – **Implemented**
- **Goal**: Allow admins to export attendance data collected via QR scans.
- **Details**:
  - Export formats: CSV and Excel.
  - Date‑range selector for the export period.
  - Filters for present vs. absent entries.
  - Summary overview with total attendances, absences, and per‑user statistics.
  - Accessible from the admin dashboard under a new **Export** button.
- **Benefits**: Simplifies record‑keeping, enables further analysis, and aids reporting.
